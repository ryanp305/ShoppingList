Shopping List

Heading to the grocery store? Make a shopping list.

How it works:

Type the name of an item in the text box below and hit enter to add it to your list. Once an item has been added you can use the 'x' button next to the item in the list to remove it, or click the orange reset button below if you want to clear your entire list and start a new one.
